The case has two parts: top and bottom. They are secured through 4 m3 bolts and heat inserts. The bottom of the case has holes for the LED light to shine, as well as a tribute to my favorite movie, How to Train Your Dragon.

BOM: 
9x Cherry MX Switches
9x DSA Keycaps
4x M3x5x4 Heatset inserts
4x M3x16mm SHCS Bolts
9x 1N4148 DO-35 Diodes.
8x WS2812B LEDs
1x 0.91" 128x32 OLED Display
1x EC11 Rotary Encoder
1x XIAO RP2040
1x Case (2 printed parts)
